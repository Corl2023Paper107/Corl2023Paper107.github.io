Generalizing XGX To MiniGrid Tasks

Lava

Empty

4-Rooms



In order to showcase the broader applications of XGX, we train XGX on three different tasks in MiniGrid framework: lava-crossing, empty, and 4-rooms.



Learning Curves

We find that XGX improves over our baseline of PPO across all three of these tasks. Even in the difficult lava environment, the agent is able to learn a policy that is capable of earning a non-trivial reward.
